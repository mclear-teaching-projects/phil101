Class 16 - Free Will & Alternate Possibilities

Date Mon 24 October 2016 Tags free will / determinism / coercion / responsibility / desert / alternate possibilities / compatibilism / incompatibilism

Today we continue our discussion of free will. We will look at Harry Frankfurt’s case of Jones, and whether free will really does require that one be able to do otherwise.

Slides from today’s class are here.

Readings

  • Frankfurt, Alternate Possibilities and Moral Responsibility

Reading Questions

  • What is the “principle of alternate possibilities” (PAP)?
  • How does Frankfurt’s case concerning Jones affect our assessment of the importance of PAP?
  • Is free will necessary for moral responsibility?
  • Is free will compatible with determinism?
