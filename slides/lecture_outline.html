Assumptions of the Argument

  • Premise (2): If our physical theories are highly successful then they correctly describe the nature of reality
    • Assumes that the correctness of our theories is required for/best explains their success
      • What if the success of physics is a mere coincidence?
  • Premise (4): If our physical theories correctly describe the nature of reality then physicalism is true
    • Assumes that our physical theories are exhaustive
