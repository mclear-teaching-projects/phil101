Class 10 - The Puzzle of Representation

Date Mon 26 September 2016 Tags representation / thought / language / pictures / mind / artificial intelligence

Today we’ll discuss the nature of representation, what forms such representation may take, and whether any particular form might be fundamental. We’ll see why the notion of representation is important, and why it has also seemed so puzzling.

Slides from today are here.

Readings

  • Crane, The Puzzle of Representation

Reading Questions

  • What is the puzzle of representation?
  • In what ways does linguistic representation differ from pictorial representation?
  • What kind(s) of representation does Crane think fundamental?
